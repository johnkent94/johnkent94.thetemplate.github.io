Who we Are

Restoring Men Unto The Ancient path

Our Vision. Our Mission. Our Commission.

With great pleasure and Jesus joy, I welcome you to this site. The Template is a God-inspired initiative that focuses on creating a platform for the new move of the Holy Spirit in our time - It is a known fact that as time changes, different earthly events, at global, national and communal levels are unfolding posing daunting challenges to our Christian faith and persuasions..

In time as this, we need a fresh move of the Holy Spirit in providing insights on how to navigate our path and surmount these challenges. As the unchanging Changer, the Spirit of God has chosen this platform for the release of timely and relevant WORD of life that will:


- Translate and transform our situations,
- Provide the needed wisdom to effectively navigate our changing world
- Provide a stand upon which our faith can be built as we grow into the fullness of His image
- And ultimately through us creating the needed change in our world as we expect the second return of Our Lord Jesus Christ.
YOU ARE WELCOME!
Lekan & Funke Adebayo

Our Committment
Committments are...

This website is yet another avenue for more people to have access to this word of truth in pursuance of our God-given mandate.
